About Me

before i started my U.X. design journey, i was an actor in Los Angeles. i've included one of my old headshots to prove it.

i moved there to try my hand at acting after graduating from college in 2015.

after four years, three part-time jobs, and two punctured tires, i decided to make a change.

i was disappointed by the lack of empathy i found among my peers in show business. ultimately, this was why i decided to make a change.

i already had experience working in editing projects, sound design, live performances, and videography. it was clear that i already had an aptitude for communication, connection, and technology.

that's why U.X. Design is the perfect path for my skillset.

i don't claim to be a coding expert, nor a U.X. expert. i know i have a lot to learn.

what i do know is that empathy is what lies at the heart of our industry.

what drives our industry is human connection and understanding.

what drives our industry is the heart.
