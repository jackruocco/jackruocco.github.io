Data Management

as a part of my core curriculum at Northwestern, we had to take a class on data management.

data strategy, cybersecurity, basic SQL, and data stewardship were all a part of the course.

near the end of the term, we were tasked with creating a fake movie trailer. the story had to be based on our own, real-world data analysis.

my project focused on a relevant issue at the time – how remote learning had affected the scholastic achievement of college students during the COVID pandemic.

here is the trailer! my own family couldn't stop laughing, so i hope you enjoy it, too 😆
