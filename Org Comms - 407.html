Communications in Orgs

one of my first courses in my new Master's program focused on my favorite topic of all – user experience design!

in a term-long project (10 weeks), i was part of a team that researched and developed a prototype for a new travel app.

our only prompt? "make an app for someone that wants to explore a city."

our team discovered a need for sports fans who follow their teams to their "away" games. fans needed navigation, tips on local bars and restaurants, lodging, and even the most efficient ways to get in and out of the sports venues.

our solution? "FieldDay," the app for the travelling fan.

initially, we thought about adding other stuff, like V.R. replicas of old stadiums, virtual tickets, and online chatrooms with other fans.
