App Design

i was grateful that one of the first courses i took at Northwestern was about U.X. design.


in a term-long project (10 weeks), i was part of a team project tasked with designing something for tourists.


our only prompt? "make an app for someone that wants to explore a city."


our team discovered a need from travelling sports fans. these die-hards follow their favorite teams to "away" games in different cities, no matter how far.

they needed help with all the issues that came with travel, plus the issues that came with large events.

parking, hotels, restaurants, flights – it all needed to be accounted for.


our solution? "FieldDay," the app for the travelling fan.


FieldDay was positively received by our test subjects, as well as our instructor.


if we had more time, we would have loved to have taken the prototype to another round of testing. sadly, the quarter ended before we could.
